1. Fix AI vs Modeling Cost logic

Current dashboard output appears inconsistent: AI / LLM usage and modeling cost can be double-counted or conflict with total cost. Fix this.

Use:
- total_cost = sum(receipt["cost"] for all receipts)
- ai_cost = sum cost where category is "llm" or "ai_support"
- modeling_cost = sum cost where category is "modeling"
- other_cost = total_cost - ai_cost - modeling_cost

Do not double-count.
If other_cost is near zero but slightly negative due to floating point, clamp to 0.

Observation logic:
- If ai_cost > modeling_cost:
  "You spent more on AI assistance than on model training."
- Else:
  "AI assistance was a smaller portion of your total compute cost."
