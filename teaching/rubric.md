# Rubric: Cost-Aware Computing Assignment

Total: 100 points

## Cost Tracking: 20 Points

- 20: Uses CCS consistently for all major actions and includes clear receipts.
- 15: Tracks most major actions, with minor omissions.
- 10: Tracks some actions but leaves important costs out.
- 5: Mentions cost but does not use receipts clearly.
- 0: Does not address cost.

## Pre-Run Estimation And Decision-Making: 20 Points

- 20: Estimates expensive actions before running and uses estimates to decide what to run.
- 15: Includes estimates but decision-making is only partly explained.
- 10: Estimates are present but not connected to choices.
- 5: Estimates appear after execution only.
- 0: No pre-run estimation.

## Cost-Performance Analysis: 20 Points

- 20: Compares performance, cost, cost per metric, marginal gain, and marginal cost.
- 15: Compares performance and cost with limited marginal analysis.
- 10: Reports cost and performance separately.
- 5: Reports only one side of the tradeoff.
- 0: No meaningful comparison.

## Final Recommendation: 20 Points

- 20: Recommends a workflow with a clear, evidence-based tradeoff argument.
- 15: Recommendation is reasonable but missing some evidence.
- 10: Recommendation is stated but weakly justified.
- 5: Recommendation is unclear.
- 0: No recommendation.

## Reflection On Reproducibility And Scale: 20 Points

- 20: Explains whether the workflow is reproducible and projects cost at larger scale.
- 15: Addresses reproducibility or scale well, but not both.
- 10: Mentions reproducibility or scale briefly.
- 5: Reflection is vague.
- 0: No reflection.
