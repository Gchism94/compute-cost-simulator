# Assignment Template: Budget-Constrained Computing

## Goal

Use Compute Cost Simulator to compare at least three approaches under a fixed
simulated compute budget. Your final recommendation must use both performance
and cost evidence.

All costs are simulated pedagogical estimates, not real billing.

## Scenario

You have a simulated budget of `$_____` to complete a computing task. Your task
may involve model training, inference, LLM prompting, RAG, AI-assisted coding,
or a combination of these.

## Requirements

- Run or simulate at least three approaches.
- Track each action with CCS receipts.
- Include at least one pre-run cost estimate before an expensive action.
- Report one performance or usefulness metric for each approach.
- Compare cost, performance, and cost per metric.
- Explain which workflow you recommend and why.
- Discuss whether another student could reproduce your workflow within the same budget.

## Suggested Workflow

1. Start with a cheap baseline.
2. Try one incremental improvement.
3. Estimate an expensive option before running it.
4. Decide whether to run, skip, or reduce the expensive option.
5. Track any LLM, RAG, or AI assistant support costs.
6. Summarize receipts and make a final recommendation.

## Submission

Submit:

- Your code or notebook.
- A receipt summary.
- A short recommendation memo.
- Answers to the reflection questions assigned by your instructor.
